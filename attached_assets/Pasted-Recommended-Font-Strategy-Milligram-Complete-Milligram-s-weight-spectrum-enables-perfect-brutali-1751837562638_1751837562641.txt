## Strategic Advantages of Milligram-Only:

**Visual Consistency:** Single font family eliminates typographic discord while providing massive weight variation for clear hierarchy.

**Operational Clarity:** Uniform character shapes across all weights ensure rapid text recognition under operational stress—critical for healthcare transportation interfaces.

**Load Performance:** One font family reduces HTTP requests and improves initial page load compared to multiple font imports.

**Design Authority:** Milligram’s geometric construction aligns perfectly with brutalist architectural principles—clean, uncompromising, functional.

## Weight Mapping for Interface Elements:

- **900 (Black):** Page titles, system alerts
- **800 (Extra Bold):** Section headers, primary actions
- **700 (Bold):** Card titles, navigation active states
- **600 (Semi Bold):** Button labels, form labels
- **500 (Medium):** Body text, descriptions
- **400 (Regular):** Secondary text, placeholders
- **300 (Light):** Timestamps, metadata (minimal use)
